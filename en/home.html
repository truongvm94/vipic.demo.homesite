ABOUT VIPIC

Vietnam Industrial Park Investment Joint Stock Company was granted a business registration license (12th change) by the Hanoi Department of Planning and Investment in 2024. The company is a unit with extensive experience in the field. real estate sector, especially industrial real estate. Currently, the Company is the Investor of a number of Industrial Park and Cluster projects, of which the key project is Industrial Park No. 3 - Nghi Son Economic Zone, which was granted an Investment Registration Certificate in July 2024. , with a total area of ​​247 hectares.

KEY PROJECTS - NGHI SON IZ NO. 3

Industrial Park (Industrial Park) No. 3 Nghi Son - Thanh Hoa, granted an Investment Certificate on July 15, 2024 and operated by Nghi Son Industrial Park No. 3 Infrastructure Investment and Exploitation Company Limited, is a project important project in the economic development strategy of Thanh Hoa province. The formation and development of industrial park No. 3 of Nghi Son economic zone contributes to the growth of the province's economy, successfully implementing the industrialization and modernization program, creating favorable conditions for the investment environment. investment, attracting investment in production and socio-economic development.
