Nghi Son IZ no.3

I - GENERAL INTRODUCTION

Nghi Son IZ No. 3 - Thanh Hoa, which was granted the Investment Certificate on October 6, 2016 and is operated and developed by VIPIC, is an important project in the economic development strategy of Thanh Hoa province. The establishment and development of industrial zone No. 3 - Nghi Son economic zone contributes to economic growth of the province, successfully implementing the industrialization and modernization program, creating favorable conditions for the environmental investment, production development and socio-economic development.

Nghi Son IZ No. 3 is located in the southern part of Thanh Hoa province, on the north-south axis of the country, connecting the northern region with the central region, with the southern Laos and northeastern Thailand markets. Nghi Son IZ No. 3 is about 200km from the south of Hanoi capital, potential to develop into the largest seaport in Vietnam. This is one of five key national projects with the goal of building a multi-sector economic zone, focusing on favorable conditions and incentives to attract investment in the field of industrial production of various types of public manufacturing mechanics, precision engineering, electrical equipment, construction machinery, medical equipment, consumer goods, apparel.

2. Infrastructure, Utilities and Trusted Services

1.1 Electricity

Our electricity distribution system is built on the core criteria of reliability and ensuring the continuity for customers' business activities:

- Power source: Building 22kV line from 110/22kV transformer station of Industrial Zone No. 5 to supply electricity for industrial parks.

- Total capacity: 38,000 kVA.

- Distribution: stable and reliable underground cable system.

- Components: LS cable, ABB switch.

- Quality control: 3-level security system, 24/7 monitoring, ensuring quick isolation of incidents and minimizing waiting time.

1.2 Water and Wastewater

- Clean water capacity: 7,300 m3/day.

- Can supply demineralized water, steam, purified water according to customer needs.

- Waste water treatment capacity: 3,800 m3/day, upgradable to 24,000 m3/day.

- It is the only industrial zone in the north of Vietnam with a wastewater treatment plant meeting ISO 9001 and 14001 standards. On-site pre-treatment facilities can be provided on demand.

- Quality control: 3-level security system, 24/7 monitoring, ensuring quick isolation of incidents and minimizing waiting time.

1.3 Fire water

- Fire hose system surrounds the entire industrial park.

- Underground, closed HDPE pipe network.

- Pressure: 5-7 bar.

- Capacity: 324 l/s.

1.4 Surface drainage system

Underground concrete pipe network and open ditch with trapdoor.

Capacity: 77,000 m3.

1.5 Maintenance and security
