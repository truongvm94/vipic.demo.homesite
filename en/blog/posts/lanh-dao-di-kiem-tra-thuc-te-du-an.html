Leaders go to reality check the project

Đăng ngày Tuesday, 27/April/2021 bởi Quản trị viên

On April 27, 2021, after solemnly holding the 2021 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders in Nghi Son town, Thanh Hoa province where the Company participated in investing and building Industrial Park No. 3 with an area of 247ha , belonging to Nghi Son Economic Industrial Park, the delegates and shareholders, after attending the meeting, went down to inspect the construction site of Nghi Son Industrial Park No. 3 to check the project implementation process.

The Company's leadership requested the project unit to strictly implement the site clearance in accordance with the commitment with the Provincial People's Committee and committed to prepare enough capital to implement the project.
