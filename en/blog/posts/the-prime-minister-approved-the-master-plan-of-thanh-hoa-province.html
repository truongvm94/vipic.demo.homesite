The Prime Minister approved the master plan of Thanh Hoa province

Đăng ngày Wednesday, 01/March/2023 bởi Quản trị viên

On February 27, 2023, on behalf of the Prime Minister, Deputy Prime Minister Tran Hong Ha signed Decision No. 153/QD-TTg approving the master plan of Thanh Hoa province for the period of 2021-2030, with a vision to 2045.
The planning of Thanh Hoa province in the period of 2021-2030, with a vision to 2045, will turn Thanh Hoa into a new growth pole, along with Hanoi, Hai Phong and Quang Ninh forming a development quadrangle in the North of the country.

Accordingly, the proposed plan is that Thanh Hoa province will be self-reliant, self-sufficient, innovative, creative, attract talents, improve the quality of human resources and capacity for international economic integration... The goal is to by 2030, strive to turn Thanh Hoa into one of the major regional and national centers of heavy industry, energy industry, processing and manufacturing; large-scale agriculture, high added value; logistics services, tourism, education and training, specialized health care, culture and sports. By 2045, to become a rich, beautiful, civilized and modern province; comprehensively developed province and model of the whole country.

Regarding the development of sectors and fields, the province will focus on the development direction of important industries of the province, considering it as a development pillar. Specifically, with the processing and manufacturing industry, it will focus on developing Thanh Hoa to become one of the major centers of the North Central region and the whole country in terms of processing and manufacturing industry. With agriculture, restructuring the agricultural industry, the focus is on developing large-scale, high-value-added models of cultivation and husbandry, closely linked with the food processing value chain to improve the quality of life. High production efficiency. With tourism, the highlight is that by 2030, Thanh Hoa will become one of the major tourism centers of the country with high-quality tourism products, affirming its brand and competitiveness...

The plan to build 5 inter-district zones and develop 4 dynamic economic centers and economic corridors; the plan for development of difficult and extremely difficult areas with the goal by 2030, to plan, arrange and arrange 100% of households living in areas affected by natural disasters and scattered households. , free migrants in mountainous districts.

Regarding the urban development plan, by 2025, the whole province has 47 urban centers of all kinds, of which 1 city is a grade I city, 2 is a grade III, 1 is a grade IV, and 43 is a grade V. By 2030, there will be 47 urban centers, of which 1 city will be grade I, 2 grade III, 4 grade IV; established 3 new towns including: Tho Xuan, Hoang Hoa, Quang Xuong; 40 urban centers of grade V. The rural territory organization plan is associated with the urbanization process of the province and socio-economic characteristics of each locality.

As for the plan to organize space for development of economic zones, industrial parks and industrial clusters: Focus on developing Nghi Son economic zone into one of the key coastal urban - industrial and service centers of the whole country. After 2030, develop Na Meo international border gate into Na Meo border gate economic zone. Continue to implement 8 industrial parks according to the approved planning with a total area of 1,424.2 ha. New development of 9 industrial parks with a total area of 2,281.5 ha. After 2030, develop two new industrial parks with an area of 872 hectares. By 2030, the province will have 115 industrial clusters with a total area of 5,267.25 hectares. The period after 2030 includes 126 industrial clusters with a total area of 5,893.65 ha.

Previously, at the Conference on Appraisal of the Thanh Hoa Provincial Planning for the period 2021-2030, with a vision to 2045 taking place on May 27, 2022 in Hanoi, the Master Plan was approved by all members of the Appraisal Council. The evaluation aspect is to closely follow the provisions of the Planning Law and the orientations on the national master plan, as well as the orientation on planning and regional planning.
